Each Imagination Vacation CD contains two journeys, each about thirty minutes long. Just one audio sample isn’t enough!

Track 1: Floating on a River

Starts with a fun movement sequence to help wound-up kids make the transition to calm so they can de-stress and relax.

1. getting started
2. along the way
3. we’ve arrived

Track 2: Twilight by the Lake

Crickets chirping, water rippling, fireflies dancing … real bedtime help for those “I can’t sleep!” nights.

1. introduction
2. fireflies
3. the stars

Imagination Vacation is designed for kids, but it works for grown-ups, too!
